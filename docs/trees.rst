Decision Trees
"""""""""""""""""""""""""

Advantages
------------
* Easy to interpret.
* Efficient inference. Inference time is proportional to the depth of the tree, not its size.
* No risk of local optima during training. 

Disadvantages
--------------
* Can easily overfit.
* Requires a quantity of data that is exponential in the depth of the tree.
